Parashah Big Ideas

age-friendly

Ki Teitzei has many mitzvot. This game focuses on student-friendly themes: responsibility, returning lost objects, safety, kindness to animals, dignity for workers, honest measures, and remembering what happened with Amalek.

Audio Options

browser-based

Use Hear It buttons for browser pronunciation support. Use Read Aloud for microphone checking in Chrome or Edge. When speech recognition is unavailable, the game switches to click-order and self-check verification.

Hebrew Power-Ups

Read each word from right to left. Tap “practice” to hear, chunk, and type it. The typed check ignores vowels so the learner can prove recognition without needing a Hebrew keyboard with niqqud.
