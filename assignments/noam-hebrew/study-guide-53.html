Quick Grammar Clues

m.s. means masculine singular.

f.s. means feminine singular.

ב... means in / at. The dots show that a place can come after it.

Watch the tiny difference: קורא = read (m.s.) and קוראת = read (f.s.). The extra ת helps show feminine singular.
